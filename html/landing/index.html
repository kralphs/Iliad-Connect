Save Time

It only takes a couple minutes to manually download a file from Odyssey and attach it to your matter in Clio. But that can easily add up to fifteen or more hours over the course of a month. With Iliad Connect, all you need to do is set a custom field to the Odyssey case number, and we will take care of it from there.

Easy to Use

It takes less than ten minutes to get your account set up. We even help you assign Odyssey case numbers to your matters. Our add-ons for your email service can help you search matters in Clio and assign the case number from a selected e-mail. These add-ons integrate with both your mobile and desktop inbox so you can take care of things no matter where you are.

Secure

We understand that we are asking you to trust us with your sensitive information. But you can feel at ease knowing that we never log or even temporarily store any personal information or documents. All communications are encrypted and shared only with trusted endpoints. Our servers are hosted on Google Cloud Platform which helps us stay secure, but should you suspect any of your credentials have been compromised, we provide a way for you to rescind any authorization you grant us until you feel ready to start again.
